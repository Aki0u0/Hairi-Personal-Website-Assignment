AMERICA, 1899. THE END OF THE WILD WEST BEGINS

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an epic tale of life in America at the dawn of the modern age. Set in 1899, the game follows Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang as they rob, fight, and flee across the heartland of the United States. With lawmen, bounty hunters, and rival gangs closing in, Arthur must choose between his own ideals and his loyalty to the gang that raised him. Experience a vast, immersive open world filled with rich storytelling, stunning visuals, and meaningful choices. From intense shootouts to quiet moments on horseback, Red Dead Redemption 2 is more than just a western — it’s a masterpiece of interactive storytelling.
