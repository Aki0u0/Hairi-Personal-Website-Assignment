Yakuza 0 is a gritty action-adventure set in the neon-lit streets of 1980s Japan. Dive into the origin stories of Kazuma Kiryu and Goro Majima as they rise through the ranks of the criminal underworld during Japan’s economic boom. Caught in a power struggle over a patch of land, they must fight for survival, honor, and redemption.

With a blend of intense street brawling, heartfelt storytelling, and over-the-top side activities — from karaoke to real estate — Yakuza 0 delivers an unforgettable mix of drama, chaos, and charm. It’s the perfect entry point into the legendary Yakuza series.
